**Batch 1 (single-leg validation set):** 1× hip bracket, 1× upper link, 1× lower link, 1× foot — total est. print time ≈1.8–2.3 hr, est. mass ≈21 g (excludes servo/bearing hardware).

**Batch 2 (remaining structure):** 3× of each leg part + body + battery mount + electronics mount — total est. print time ≈8–9 hr.

## Success Criteria
A builder can start Batch 1 printing today without asking a clarifying question — orientation, supports, and material are specified for every part in the first leg module.
